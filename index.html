Quick family guide

Zoe top stack: Beauty and the Beast, Pooh, Monsters, Harmony in Color, one big character moment.
Miles sweet spot: Small World, Jungle Cruise, Mickey’s PhilharMagic, parade rhythm, character greetings.
Sofia strategy: Prioritize Baby OK items during tired or meltdown mode, then use Parent Split only when really worth it.
